Touching base regarding PA denials for pharmacists. Effective
immediately, when **denying a case**, please change the day’s supply to
1 instead of leaving as the requested dates of service (Ex:
2/7/23-2/7/24 for 1 year approval). We have been advised to do this to
prevent issues with dates overlapping with potential future iPA’s for
the same GCNSeqNo. Eventually, technicians will be updating the date to
a 1 day supply prior to sending to MEDREVIEW, but this will not be
completed until later in the week. In the event that the pharmacist
**approves** out of MEDREVIEW, it will be **<span class="underline">our
responsibility to update the day’s supply, daily dose, quantity
requested and approval dates</span>** prior to sending the approval
letter.
